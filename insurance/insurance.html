Common insurance questions

Does being listed mean my visit is automatically covered?

Not always. Coverage can depend on your exact plan, deductible, copay, provider availability, service type, authorization requirements, and state. Please verify your benefits before beginning services.

What information should I have ready?

Have your insurance card, member ID, date of birth, state of residence, and the type of service you are requesting. This helps the team review your coverage more efficiently.

What if my insurance is not listed?

You may still reach out. Quantum Behavioral Health & Wellness may be able to discuss self-pay options, referrals, future plan updates, or other next steps based on your needs.
